--- layout: post title: The Hustle --- ~ How to get a development job after bootcamp

Dec. 01, 2016

What a darling question, the things one must do in order to procure the dream job in development after completing one's bootcamp program. There are several bloggy resources out there dictating how the wonder developer got his job.

Let me save you some time and sum it up for you. Do the hustle.

Still need further explanation? I shall define the hustle for you - apply to every single job you possibly can. That is basically it. Getting the wonder job is just as difficult as you think it will be - which is to say, no one is going to do it for you.
