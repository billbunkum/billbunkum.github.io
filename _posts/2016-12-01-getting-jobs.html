How do I get a development job?

What a darling question, the things one must do in order to procure the dream job in development after completing one's bootcamp program. There are several
  • Andrew Chalkley
  • Karim Elsheikh
 resources out there dictating how the wonder developer got his job.

Let me save you some time and sum it up for you. Do the hustle.

Still need further explanation? I shall define what doing the hustle requires of you - apply to every single job you possibly can. That is basically it. Getting the wonder job is just as difficult as you think it will be - which is to say, no one is going to do it for you.

Harsh Truths

You might be thinking, 'dear me, that wasn't much help'; in fact, you are incorrect, good sir or madame. If you do not believe me, go here and see a real example of some self-obvious advice. Or, how about here for some extra useful blather.

It is a good thing for people to be honest with you (especially your mentors at bootcamp). You have to hustle; do not expect it to be easy; keep learning, coding and developing projects on your own (or with others).

It is pretty simple when you think about it.
